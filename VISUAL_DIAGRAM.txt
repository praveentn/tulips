═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                    WHY ADDITION-ONLY WORKS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Traditional Neural Network:
    output = Σ(weight_i × input_i) + bias
             └─ MULTIPLICATION ─┘

Our Model:
    score = Σ(param[feature][label])
            └── ADDITION ONLY ──┘

Why it's equivalent:
• Our "params" are like weights
• Our features are binary (present/absent)
• So multiplication by 1 or 0 is same as:
  - Including the parameter (if feature present)
  - Not including it (if feature absent)

Example:
    Traditional: weight_YOU × 1 + weight_GO × 1 + weight_LEFT × 1
    Our model:   param[YOU] + param[GO] + param[LEFT]
                           └──── Same result! ────┘

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                         PRACTICAL EXAMPLE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Question: "Will 'YOU TAKE RIGHT' be classified as COMMAND?"

Step 1: Extract features
    • YOU
    • TAKE  
    • RIGHT
    • YOU_TAKE
    • TAKE_RIGHT

Step 2: Look up scores (after training)
    COMMAND:     YOU(+5) + TAKE(+2) + RIGHT(0) + YOU_TAKE(+3) = +10
    STATEMENT:   YOU(-3) + TAKE(-1) + RIGHT(0) + YOU_TAKE(-2) = -6
    CONFIRMATION: all zeros = 0

Step 3: Pick highest
    max(+10, -6, 0) = +10 → COMMAND ✓
